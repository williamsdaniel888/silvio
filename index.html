Silvio: Neither Private Nor Public

The best defence against a state-sponsored counterfeiting industrial complex is multilateral sanctioning from the grassroots upwards. I'm going to be controversial and say Bitcoin should be privatized.

It would be interesting to tie the supply of money to the physical ownership of a perishable token, because that would ensure a minimum circulation of money without inflationary pressures.

Alternatively the decentralized distributed ledger of the Yapese people should be explored further, as physical ownership of value was imputed through community recognition rather than actual possession.

Along the lines of freigeld, maybe a soluble disc of sodium chloride containing quantum dots could be used as a checksum against the integrity of the token.

Furthermore the solubility of the checkdisquettes would build in a natural rate of deflation while also allowing local economies to literally remain solvent.

The token should be called Silvio in honor of Silvio Gesell, and the community behind it could be called Gesellschaft (community in German).
